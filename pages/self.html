Breathing Exercises

Simple breathing techniques to calm your mind and body instantly

4-7-8 Breathing

Breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Activates relaxation response.

How to Practice:

  1. Find a comfortable position
  2. Inhale through nose for 4 counts
  3. Hold breath for 7 counts
  4. Exhale through mouth for 8 counts
  5. Repeat 3-4 times

Box Breathing

Breathe in square pattern: 4 seconds in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold. Excellent for focus.

How to Practice:

  1. Visualize a square as you breathe
  2. Inhale while tracing up one side (4 counts)
  3. Hold while tracing across top (4 counts)
  4. Exhale while tracing down other side (4 counts)
  5. Hold while tracing bottom (4 counts)
  6. Complete 4-8 cycles

Diaphragmatic Breathing

Deep belly breathing that reduces stress and improves oxygenation. Great for anxiety relief.

How to Practice:

  1. Lie on your back with knees bent
  2. Place one hand on chest, other on belly
  3. Breathe so belly rises, not chest
  4. Practice for 5-10 minutes daily

Grounding Techniques

Stay present and connected to your body during overwhelming moments

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Engage all 5 senses to bring yourself back to the present moment during anxiety or panic.

Practice Steps:

  1. Name 5 things you can see
  2. Name 4 things you can touch
  3. Name 3 things you can hear
  4. Name 2 things you can smell
  5. Name 1 thing you can taste

Feet on Ground

Feel your connection to the earth to reduce anxiety and feel more stable and centered.

Practice Steps:

  1. Stand or sit with feet flat on floor
  2. Focus on sensations in your feet
  3. Notice temperature, pressure, texture
  4. Imagine roots growing from feet into ground
  5. Practice for 2-3 minutes

Body Scan

Systematically bring awareness to different parts of your body to release tension and relax deeply.

Practice Steps:

  1. Start with toes and work upward
  2. Notice sensations without judgment
  3. Breathe into each body part
  4. Release tension as you exhale
  5. Complete full body scan in 10-20 minutes

Mindfulness Exercises

Cultivate present-moment awareness and reduce stress through focused attention

Mindful Meditation

Focus attention on breath, thoughts, and sensations without judgment. Builds emotional regulation.

Getting Started:

  • Find a quiet, comfortable space
  • Set timer for 5-10 minutes
  • Focus on your breath as an anchor
  • When mind wanders, gently return to breath
  • Practice daily for best results

Mindful Observation

Notice your surroundings with fresh eyes, as if seeing them for the first time. Enhances awareness.

Practice Steps:

  • Choose an object in your environment
  • Observe it with curiosity, not judgment
  • Notice colors, shapes, textures, shadows
  • Observe your reactions to the object
  • Practice for 2-3 minutes

Gratitude Practice

Regularly reflect on things you're grateful for. Shifts focus from negative to positive aspects of life.

Daily Practice:

  • Write down 3 things you're grateful for
  • Be specific about why you're grateful
  • Include small pleasures and big blessings
  • Review your list weekly
  • Share gratitude with others when possible
